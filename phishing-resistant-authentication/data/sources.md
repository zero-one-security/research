## Notes

- NSM is the load-bearing, authoritative claim and is primary.
- The Microsoft "8×" figure is the vendor's measurement of its own sign-ins, not
  an independent benchmark. Cite it as a vendor figure.
- The 4.7M / ~97% figure is FIDO's framing of BankID population reach. It does not
  assert FIDO-key usage by that population; it supports only that proving identity
  with a possession is already a widespread Norwegian habit.
