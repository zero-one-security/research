## Notes

- The Storting figure is an administrative **fine** (Datatilsynet), not an
  incident cost. It is the regulator's own published decision, so it is primary,
  but it is not comparable in kind to the AKVA and Hydro cost figures.
- Initial-access paths are **not** publicly settled for these incidents. The
  LockerGoga and Conti attributions describe the malware, not how credentials
  were obtained. We do not claim a phished password as the root cause of any of
  these.
