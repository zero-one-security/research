# Sources

Every figure used in this study, with its primary source and tier.

## Cited figures

| Figure | Value | Year | Tier | Source |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Social-engineering loss, H1 2024 | NOK 360 million | 2024 | Primary | Finanstilsynet, Svindelstatistikk første halvår 2024 (18 November 2024). https://www.finanstilsynet.no/publikasjoner-og-analyser/svindel-og-svindelstatistikk/2024/h1/svindelstatistikk-forste-halvar-2024/ |
| Social-engineering loss, H2 2024 | NOK 307 million | 2024 | Primary | Finanstilsynet, Svindelstatistikk andre halvår 2024 (14 May 2025). https://www.finanstilsynet.no/publikasjoner-og-analyser/svindel-og-svindelstatistikk/2024/h2/svindelstatistikk-andre-halvar-2024 |
| Social-engineering loss, 2024 total | NOK 667 million | 2024 | Derived | Sum of the two Finanstilsynet half-year figures above (360 + 307). |

## Notes

- Both half-year figures are Finanstilsynet's own published fraud statistics
  (primary). The annual total is a plain sum we computed, not a separately
  published figure, so it is tagged "derived".
- The figures cover loss across the Norwegian financial system. They are not
  scoped to any single firm or profession.
